[The disaster that befell Earth known as the Star Stream has begun.]

Kim Dokja stared at the floating blue window before him. He had read these exact words a thousand times in the novel "Three Ways to Survive in a Ruined World", but seeing them in reality was something else entirely.

The clock read noon, but the sky outside was dark. Around him, his subway car had been transplanted into an unfamiliar, broken landscape. Other passengers stumbled out of the wreckage in confusion.

A new message appeared: [Scenario Quest #1 - Survive the first trial.]

Dokja took a breath. He alone knew how this story would unfold, and he intended to use every scrap of that knowledge to stay alive.
