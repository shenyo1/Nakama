Only I know the end of this world.

One day our protagonist, Kim Dokja, found that the web novel he had been reading for ten years, Three Ways to Survive in a Ruined World, had become reality. As the sole reader who had read the novel to its very end, he now held knowledge of the apocalypse and the secrets that could change its course.

Together with his companions, Dokja must navigate a world where scenarios descend and constellations watch from above, betting on the fates of mortals for entertainment.
